My research seeks to identify and better understand the genetic contributors to complex traits, particularly disorders of the brain, by making use of rich sets of “big data” in silico. My goal is to increase knowledge on the genetic aetiology of complex diseases to allow for earlier and better diagnoses and more personalized treatment. I also transmit this information through teaching.
